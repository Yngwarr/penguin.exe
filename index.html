- How to play -

* Use mouse to move penguins and files to bin.
* Don't run out of CPU or RAM.

- Credits -

* Yngvarr - programming, sounds of penguins.
* Syudzius - all the art in the game.

* Music - 'Boiler' and 'Moody Dungeon' by Joshua McLean (via https://retroindiejosh.itch.io/music-pack-13).
* SFX_WriteCrumpleThrow by Perel (via https://freesound.org/people/Perel/sounds/173440/, cut).
